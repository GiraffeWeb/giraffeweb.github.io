Scheme of work

We create products that help grow your business

1. Discussion of the project, filling out the brief.

We discuss your project by phone, skype or in correspondence. We select the best tariff for you and we answer your questions. To get started, you need to fill out a brief. How quickly and constructively we understand your requirements for the website depends on how it is filled out.
Download brief

2. Individual plan.

We thoroughly research our client's business, services and products in order to clearly understand the business potential and target audience of the future project. This will be the key to defining functional and correct requirements. We design an individual strategy to get the most out of the conversion.

3.Development of a prototype and text.

The prototype and text are developed depending on the chosen tariff. The prototype is the "skeleton" of the future site, its schematic representation with all sorts of elements: buttons, text, menus, application forms. Within 3 working days after the start of work, we provide a prototype with a text for approval. After receiving the prepayment of the project, you can make edits, depending on the selected plan, which will be made on the site.

4. Design development.

Ыite design, work with color, font, graphics. It is created based on the completed brief, competitor analysis and your views. The design is developed within 5 working days, during this time, we present the project. Our professional team thoroughly reviews every page to make sure all features work perfectly on every page.

5. Full website optimization.

This is followed by the connection of the URL, the necessary tools, such as a blog, a feedback form, setting up site scripts. It connects the main services of Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager for the complete indexing of your website on search engines! The last payment of the project is paid. Our work only ends if the client is satisfied.

6. Start!

Our experts offer the client training in using the web panel, setting up the site. We are always happy to help clients to smoothly advance their project, so we have technical support.

Services

We create high-load, fault-tolerant websites that improve the business performance of our clients. Even in the most technologically complex projects, we are committed to the principle of non-toxicity: only clean design, as well as the desire to simplify the UX to the point, for which the user came to the site.
Deep understanding of the user is the main tool of our work. We focus on analytics and rapid prototyping, continuous usability testing and iterative development. The result is the very user experience that determines the effectiveness and usefulness of a web product.
